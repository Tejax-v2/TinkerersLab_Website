Who We Are

We are engineers by choice!

Welcome to Tinkerers' Lab! We are a dedicated space for all the passionate tinkerers out there. Our lab is designed to provide a creative and collaborative environment where ideas thrive and innovation flourishes. Equipped with state-of-the-art tools and resources, we empower individuals of all ages to explore, experiment, and bring their imaginative concepts to life. Through hands-on workshops, classes, and a vibrant community, we aim to nurture a love for tinkering, ignite curiosity, and inspire the next generation of inventors and problem solvers. Join us at Tinkerers' Lab and embark on an exciting journey of discovery and creation.

Our Robotics Community

The Robotics community of IIT Patna is known for its remarkable helpfulness, always extending a hand to assist and support fellow members. Their collaborative spirit and dedication foster a thriving environment for learning and innovation in the field of robotics.

Team Phoenix

ABU Robocon

Team Phoenix from IIT Patna participates in ABU Robocon annually, showcasing their robotic prowess by tackling complex problem statements with ingenuity and skill.
